Who We Are

About Cydah Network Services LLC.

A low-voltage technology design firm focused on environments where technology directly affects the care, safety, and experience of the people inside.

Our Focus

Translating care models into buildable technology design.

Cydah Network Services LLC is a low-voltage technology design firm focused on senior living and healthcare environments, with additional experience in corporate, transportation, and public safety projects. Operating under the domain cydahtelecom.com, we help owners, architects, and IT teams translate care models and operational goals into practical, buildable technology designs.

Our work emphasizes clear documentation, responsive coordination, and an understanding that technology systems are not just devices — they are part of the experience for residents, families, clinicians and staff.

We integrate into your project team from Schematic Design through Construction Administration, working alongside architects, MEP engineers, and IT partners to keep low-voltage systems coordinated, documented, and ready for contractors to execute in the field.

Vendor-neutral by practice, our recommendations are driven by what best serves the owner's long-term operational goals — not product relationships or preferred vendors.
