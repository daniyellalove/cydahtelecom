What We Do

Low-voltage technology services.

Support from early planning through construction administration, with clear drawings and proactive coordination.

01

Low-Voltage Systems Design

Structured cabling, network rooms, and low-voltage systems that align with owner standards and technology roadmaps.

  • Structured cabling & outlet layouts
  • MDF/IDF room planning & rack layouts
  • Copper & fiber backbone pathways
  • Coordination with MEP and architecture
02

Wi-Fi & Network Design Coordination

AP layouts and coordination based on predictive designs provided by IT or wireless partners, clearly documented.

  • AP placement on RCP/technology plans
  • Coordination with predictive heatmaps
  • Pathways and PoE considerations
  • Back-of-house and resident-facing coverage
03

Security, Access Control & Surveillance

Device layouts and infrastructure for access control and cameras that enhance safety and operations.

  • Card readers, door contacts & REX devices
  • Camera layouts for exterior & interior zones
  • Recording/storage considerations with IT
  • Perimeter and sensitive area strategies
04

Emergency Call & Life-Safety Support

Coordination of E-call and related life-safety technologies in senior living and healthcare settings.

  • Typical device layouts for resident units
  • Pull-cords, dome lights & annunciation points
  • Coordination with nurse call / E-call vendors
  • Documentation for contractors and AHJ review
05

Construction Administration

Ongoing support during construction to maintain design intent and address field conditions.

  • Submittal and shop drawing review
  • RFI responses and clarifications
  • Site walks and punchlists
  • As-built and closeout document review
06

Carrier & ISP / Cellular Coordination

Support for carrier demarc, ISP coordination and infrastructure for cellular boosting / DAS partners.

  • Demarc room and pathway requirements
  • Coordination with ISPs and carrier vendors
  • Infrastructure for cellular boosting / DAS (by others)
  • Documentation for long-term flexibility
