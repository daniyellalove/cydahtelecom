Primary Market

Senior Living & Assisted Living

Technology that supports residents, families, and staff in independent living, assisted living, and memory care communities.

  • E-call / emergency call and pull-cords
  • Wander management and elopement prevention
  • Access control for perimeter, units & sensitive spaces
  • Video surveillance for entries, parking and common areas
  • Resident-grade Wi-Fi and staff network coverage

Healthcare Facilities

Low-voltage design for clinical environments — coordinating with owners, IT, and design teams to support hospitals, clinics, imaging suites, and medical office buildings.

  • Access control and surveillance for sensitive areas
  • Integration with hospital standards and life-safety requirements
  • Coordination with nurse call, RTLS and other systems (by others)
