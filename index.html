How We Work

A clear process from concept to closeout.

01 · Discovery & Planning

Understand your environment.

Review program requirements, owner standards, and existing conditions to understand how technology supports operations, care models, and growth.

02 · Design (SD, DD, CD)

Develop coordinated drawings.

Develop and refine technology drawings and documentation that integrate with architectural and MEP sets, providing coordinated information for bidding and construction.

03 · Construction Support

Stay engaged through closeout.

Remain engaged through construction administration to help interpret the design, respond to field questions, and verify low-voltage systems are installed as intended.
